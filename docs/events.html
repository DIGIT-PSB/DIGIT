DIGIT Works

DIGIT Works is an ongoing series of events, including project presentations, guest speakers, and workshops, open to all! We are excited to be sharing our first series of events in 2021 entirely virtually over Zoom. Follow the Zoom registration links below to join each virtual event.

Life after DIGIT? | Thursday, April 15, 2021: 6:30 - 8pm: Zoom registration

Join our panel of new and recent graduates who will share their work and speak about their adventures finding meaningful work or exploring graduate schools. Bring your questions!

Panelists:
  • Elizabeth Musfi
  • Allison Lourens
  • Alexander VanWoert

Moderated by Profs. Joel Hunt and Elisa Beshero-Bondar.

Patricia Yahn Spring Art Show | Friday, April 16, 2021 @ 8pm: Zoom registration

Penn State's Annual Spring Art Show, in honor of Patricia Yahn, will be held virtually this year as a safety precaution to ensure the health of the Behrend community and our student artists. The Patricia Yahn Spring Art Show will feature analog media works as well as DIGIT students' digital artwork!

Senior Projects! | Wednesday, April 21, 2021: 6:30 - 8pm: Zoom registration

Two of our DIGIT 494 students lead the project presentations for spring 2021. The Modeling & Sim class will share some of their class projects, too!

  • Bianka Alexander: Text Analysis of Harry Potter Screenplays (Senior Project Presentation)
  • Mason Gossy: Invicta Game Development (Senior Project Presentation)
  • Dr. Lisa Jo Elliott: Modeling & Sim demonstration

Moderated by Profs. Elisa Beshero-Bondar and Lisa Jo Elliott.
