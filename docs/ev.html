Spring 2024 Student Digital Project Presentations

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from Digit 494 Senior Capstone projects, Advanced Digital Creations (Digit 409), Data Visualization (Digit 410), Game Development Projects (Game 480), Technical Game Development (Game 250), Large-Scale Text Analysis (Digit 210), and Electronic Music Composition (Music 458) taught by Profs. Tommy Hartung, Lisa Jo Elliott, Lauren Liebe, Pulin Agrawal, Elisa Beshero-Bondar, and Joel Hunt.
