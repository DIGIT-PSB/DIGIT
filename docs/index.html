This website holds the nexus of information, student project showcasing, and latest developments in the Digital Media, Arts, and Technology program at Penn State Erie. Whether you're a prospective DIGIT major, a current DIGIT major, or you are simply curious to learn what our DIGIT community is all about, you've come to the right place. DIGIT offers a plethora of opportunities for students and non-students alike to explore the possibilities of digital medium and become creators and leaders in the rapidly-evolving digital world.

What is DIGIT?

The Digital Media, Arts and Technology program invites students eager to become fluent in the creation, implementation, and expression of ideas and information through the digital medium. DIGIT is an interdisciplinary field which combines practical technical skill with broad liberal arts studies to prepare students to enter a world where both critical thinking skills and technical literacy are in ever-increasing demand.

We encourage students to become active in confronting the issues risen by the rapidly growing digital realm, and strive keep the human element in digital disciplines alive through creative analysis and production of digital works.
